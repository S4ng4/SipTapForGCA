Riccardo Longo

Wine Director & Sommelier

A Native of Rome, Riccardo spent the summers of his childhood in the Cilento where his Nonno taught him about viticulture on his vineyard. Over the past three decades he has personally visited and met with esteemed winemakers in all 20 regions of Italy. His mission, with our wine list, is to offer wines that tell the story of the subzone terroirs of all 20 regions of Italy.

Riccardo's Wine Cellar Selections allows guests to sample Elevated Italian terroir wines by the glass at a special price through special arrangements with the winemakers.

Riccardo's accolades include: "Eccelenze Italiane" for promotion of Italian culture abroad, America Italy Society "Italian Culture Award", Wine Spectator "Award of Excellence", Filitalia Humanitarian Award for service to the Italian community.
